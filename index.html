Dry Lab: PCA + K-Means Clustering

Differential Expression Analysis

This visualization illustrates the clustering of gene expression patterns across different timepoints, enabling us to identify genes with coordinated expression profiles over time. Through this analysis, we pinpointed targets that exhibit dynamic expression changes at key timepoints, guiding our selection of candidates for further screening. Additionally, the clustering approach facilitated the identification of potentially relevant pathways, providing insight into the underlying biological processes. This work was conducted as part of research for the Rubin Lab at Harvard University.
